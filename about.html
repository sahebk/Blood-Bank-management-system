About Us


Lifeline Donors is a non-profit organization dedicated to facilitating blood donation and providing a common platform for donors and recipients in need. We are a team of passionate volunteers committed to saving lives by ensuring the availability of safe and clean blood for those in emergency situations. Recognizing the critical importance of safe blood transfusions, we operate our own well-equipped blood bank to address the high demand for blood supply and minimize the risk of receiving blood from unsafe sources. Our organization actively promotes awareness of the importance of blood donation through blood camps and outreach programs, encouraging voluntary donations from educational institutions, industries, corporate offices, and individuals. We employ state-of-the-art equipment and procedures to safely collect, test, and store donated blood, ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety.
