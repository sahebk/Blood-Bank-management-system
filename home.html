Welcome to LifeLine Donors Organization

Our Mission

Lifeline Donors is dedicated to saving lives by promoting voluntary blood donations. We strive to ensure a stable and safe blood supply for patients in need and advocate for the importance of regular blood donations in our community.

Our Services

  • Regular blood donation drives
  • Blood donation education and awareness programs
  • Support for blood recipients and their families
  • Collaboration with healthcare providers for safe blood transfusion practices

Who can donate blood?

Most people are able to give blood, but only 4% actually do. You can donate blood if you:
  • are fit and healthy
  • weight atleast 50kg
  • are 18-70 years old
  • are over 70 and have given blood in the last two years
